Prevention

There is a bunch of laws that people would have to follow. They also have places for species-protected areas. Some Laws protect endangered species from threats. There have also been species that were reintroduced into the environment. There are also many ways individuals can help.

Laws

The Fish & Wildlife Service has an Endangered Species Act of 1973 that protects all fish, wildlife, and plants endangered. It is illegal to take, kill, harm, or harass an endangered species. Individuals are required to obtain a permit to fish or hunt any animal. This law helps the Bald Eagle to recover well. There was a danger of extinction for bald eagles. There were only a few nests left. Over the year, bald eagles are no longer in the danger zone.

Reintroduced

When a species is released into the wild from captivity, it would be considered species reintroduction. They use it not only to bring the species back but for pest control too. For example, the overpopulation of deer has been reintroduced to the wolf.

Ways to get help

Individuals get involved by posting threads online to show awareness of the situation and spread it to others. Posting these threads online could educate others but promoting them on their page and letting the world know about it! They could educate through social media, In-person, books, movies, videos, and more. They could try to protect different species from harm’s way. If there is harm done to any species, they could call the Humane Society, the fish and wildlife services, or even the police.
